How to play

The goal of "Force of the Elementals" is to stop enemies from stealing your gold.


Drag Elementals (hold-drag-drop icon in right menu) on the map to create Towers, which will defend your treasures.


There are four types of Elementals:

  • Fire, with a huge power.
  • Water, which has a graet rate.
  • Earth, that slow enemies.
  • Air, with a area range.

Upgrade your Towers and go through waves of monsters to unlock new maps.

Good Luck!

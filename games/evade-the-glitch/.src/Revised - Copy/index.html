EVADE THE GLITCH

How to Play:

A or left arrow to move left (or right when glitched)

D or right arrow to move right (or left when glitched)

You can also hide at the left or right side of the screen but....

1)You can't hide at the same side twice in a row

2)You can't hide for more than 3 seconds!

Press TAB followed by any key to start. You have 3 lives, good luck!!

Welcome four elements tower defense. Your enemy will send ever increasing waves of enemies against you. Build towers to defend your base. Build collectors to gather more of each resource. Everytime an enemy gets to the end a building loses hitpoints and will eventually be destroyed. If all buildings are destroyed then there is nothing to stand between you and the enemy and you will lose. Notice that different towers affect enemies differently.

You are the GROUND worker! Work with your partner to fix the powerline. It should only take a minute, but small mistakes add up, and big ones send you to the hospital!

Your job is to:

  • Keep the birds off the line worker with your BB gun.
  • Warn the line worker of incoming power surges. Surges are bright yellow balls coming from the left!
  • React to warnings of oncoming trains. Watch for the line worker waving frantically.

You need to watch out for:

  • Oncoming trains! React too slow and its game-over!
  • Missing birds & hitting your partner! It adds to the timer!

Controls:

  • Use mouse to shoot the birds.
  • Use keyboard to send warnings (w) and react (r).

You are the LINE worker! Work with your partner to fix the powerline. It should only take a minute, but small mistakes add up, and big ones send you to the hospital!

Your job is to:

  • Fix wires by typing the first letter of the color of the word shown.
  • Warn the ground worker of oncoming trains. Sound waves, big half-circles on the right, indicate incoming trains!
  • React to warnings of incoming surges. Warnings will come in towards the bottom.

You need to watch out for:

  • Incoming surge warnings! React too slow and its game-over!
  • Mis-identifying the colors! It adds to the timer!

Controls:

  • Use keyboard to identify the correct color (w/r/b/g/y).
  • Use mouse click buttons (below) to send warnings and react.

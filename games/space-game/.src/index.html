How to Play?

  • This is a SPACE(empty area) finding game.
  • Approximately calculate the SPACE percentage(%) of the shape.
  • Set the slider to your calculated space %.
  • Click on GUESS SPACE button.
  • You will be given a point if your Answer is close to Correct Answer.
  • Game gets over after playing for 1 minute.
  • BONUS: You will be given 2 points if you predict Correct Answer.

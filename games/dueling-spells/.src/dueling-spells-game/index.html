Dueling Spells

A typing game set in the world of magic.

How to play the game:

So, this indeed is a basic typing game. You gotta show your best typing skills to survive in this game.

  • The aim is simple... Type a spell from the list of spells and press enter to execute a spell.
  • The faster you type and execute, the quicker the health of the opponent decreases.
  • Each spell has a power attached to it. The spell with larger power will lead to decrease in larger amount of opponents' health.
  • The first spell is just practice... scoring starts form second.
  • The opponent (the dark species indeed...!!!) would continually attack you... You won't be spared if you can't keep up with them.!!!
  • So... the one who dies first, loses... so simple!
  • You can see your characters per min and words per min from the side pane... making it a real typing test too :-p

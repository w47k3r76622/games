OFF  THE  LINES

(Rotate your device for the best playing experience)

Score 20 points or more to get to the next level

How to Play

Keep the balls on the lines. If they crash into each other or reach a gap in the line, they will fall OFF THE LINES.

You have 2 types of controls:

  • Select this to stop a ball passing through (until you select it again to release it). It will turn orange when activated.

  • Select this to switch balls to the alternative line. It will turn purple when activated.

Other things to look out for:

  • These teleport the balls from one line to another. You don't have any control over these.

  • Gaps will sometimes appear due to a line-fault. Make sure the balls avoid any gaps.

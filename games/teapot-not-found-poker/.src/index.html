Paytable

  • Fours or Better - 1
  • Two Pair - 2
  • Three of a Kind - 3
  • Straight - 4
  • Flush - 6
  • Full House - 9
  • Four of a Kind - 12
  • Straight Flush - immediate win
  • Royal Flush - immediate win

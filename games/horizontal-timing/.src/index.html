Horizontal Timing

Find the path on a time-evolving board. You see each step the board will take all together and must reform the progression timeline on your own

Instructions: go from the square to the square, avoid squares. On top you see the evolution of the board each move you make. You have a limited amount of time to find the right path.
Controls:
WASD for directions
SPACEBAR to wait a move
ENTER to submit the sequence earlier.

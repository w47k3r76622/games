Scoring

  • everything is multiplied by (level + 1)
  • 1 point for every figure landed
  • 1 point for every 5 cells' distance in soft drop
  • 1 point for every 3 cells' distance in hard drop
  • 2 extra points for hard drop of distance 15 cells or more
  • 40 points for collapsing one layer
  • 100 points for collapsing two layers
  • 300 points for collapsing three layers
  • 1200 points for collapsing four layers
  • for sequential collapses, scores are multiplied by the number of the wave squared

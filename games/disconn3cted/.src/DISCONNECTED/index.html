Welcome to DISCONNECTED!

What to do?

Shoot enemies and bring all the drone's batteries to the helipad to get
it back online.

How to play?

Use z, q, d, s or arrow keys to move, left click to shoot and
right click to place traps (for tank drones). You lose a battery if you're
carrying one and an enemy shoots you. Walk over an ammobox, medkit or
battery to pick it up.

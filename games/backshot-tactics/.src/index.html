How to play

  1. Pick phase: select your 4 ships on the grid.
  2. Play phase: When your turn come, you can activate a ship
    • It moves in the active direction by 4 cells
    • It shots the nearest opponent damaging him by distance between them * 3
    • If the opponent is hit on the back side the damage is tripled
  3. Kill all the enemy ships to win

How to play

On a computer:

Jump = W or 🔼
Right = D or ▶️
Left = A or ◀️
Lay Egg = S or 🔽

On mobile:

Use on screen keypad.

General Info

Your goal is to collect the feather at the end of each level so that you can fly. You must eat bugs along the way to increase your energy. The energy bar is in the top-left. Once the bar is full, you can lay an egg to give yourself and extra boost when jumping. If you don't have enough energy and there are no more bugs, you'll have to return to the bottom of the level and walk up to the bug recharge station (bottom-right).

Good luck!

Feathers Not Found

You're a duck. Yep, a duck. Problem is, your feathers didn't come in right and you can't fly.

To catch up with your family, all of whom have just migrated, you have to collect feathers so you can finally fly.

Eat bugs to give you the energy to lay eggs for a nice boost. Avoid falling acorns and rocks. And most of all, collect those feathers!

Congratulations!

You completed the game, gathering all the feathers you need to learn to fly. Now you can join your family.

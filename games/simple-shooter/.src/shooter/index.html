How to play

Controls:

  • S(hold) - shield : This depletes over time, deactivate shield to recharge
  • D - bomb : Creates a large blast that damages all enemies that touch it, only one bomb can be activated at a time
  • CLICK(mouse) - shoot : Your primary weapon against your enemies, aim with the mouse and fire by clicking
  • CLICK+HOLD(mouse) - charge shot : Holding down the mouse button will charge your shot, your player will change color when it fully charged
  • SPACE - pause : Toggle pause

Powerups:

  • : Adds power to your shield
  • : Adds one bomb to your arsenal

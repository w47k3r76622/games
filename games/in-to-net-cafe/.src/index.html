JS13k 2020 - In To Net Cafes

CarelessLabs Twitter

  • You are the server in an Internet Cafe. Move with WASD or Arrows and Mouse to click.
  • Place a Desk, Chair and then a PC onto the table in that order or you will run out of money.
  • Press spacebar to quick select the data gun, click to fire data at a user PC when they show progress bar to earn money.
  • Fail to deliver and a user will become unhappy and eventually leave affecting your rating, a zero rating will bring no new users.
  • Build servers to generate ammo, move to the server to collect (Upgrade to auto collect).
  • Place vending machines to earn extra money.
  • Auto data turrets will deliver data for you.
  • Other ugprades include, faster shooting, more ammo per server and rating upgrades!
  • Press 1 to restart.

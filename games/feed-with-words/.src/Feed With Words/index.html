Feed with words (FwW) is a game based on word finding. The game consists in:
  • 6 questions (the receipt of candy) with a hint to the right (number of neccesary candies to feed the pot);
  • 15 groups of 2 or 3 letters (called candies);
  • Your sharp eyes to join the letters and collect those sweets;
  • Hints(3 in the beginning of game) are there to help you any time. It shows one correct answer, but only if you pass a level, you get one chance to use it.
  • And many different levels.

The purpose is to figure out all 6 answers, fill the pot with candies(letters) and pass the level.
